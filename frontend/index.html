Q: What is GPT-4o and how is it used?
A: GPT-4o is the advanced AI language model that powers ReComposer's email assistant. It analyzes your email drafts and generates improved versions with better wording, tone, and clarity, all in seconds. You get the benefit of cutting-edge AI (GPT-4 caliber) working for you.

Q: What support is included?
A: Standard plan includes email support (responses within 1 business day). Pro plan comes with priority support – you'll get responses within a few hours during business times, plus a dedicated onboarding session if needed. We're here to ensure you succeed with ReComposer.
